Bio

I cut my very first project when I was sixteen (a no-budget zombie film, of course). Amazingly, it was not the worst thing I have done since then.

The good news is that I have also gone on to do much better things too. I've worked on projects that have gone to festivals, and managed to edit for the likes of Bacardi and Acuvue. I suppose there was some good in losing all that sleep.

I like to do things well, so hit me up if you need that for your project. If you’re here by mistake, we can grab a drink or something, I guess? But you’re paying for the first round.
